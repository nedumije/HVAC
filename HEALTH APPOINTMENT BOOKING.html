Next Steps

  • 1. Prepare for your appointment

    Bring your NHS number and any relevant medical documents.

  • 2. Arrive early

    Please arrive 10 minutes before your scheduled appointment time.

  • 3. Cancel if necessary

    If you need to cancel or reschedule, please do so at least 24 hours in advance.

  • 4. Remote appointments

    If you've booked a telephone or video consultation, please be available at the scheduled time.

Need to Make Changes?

If you need to modify or cancel your appointment, you can do so by:

  • Online: Log into your NHS account
  • Phone: Call your GP practice at 01234 567890
  • In person: Visit your GP practice during opening hours
Important: Cancellations with less than 24 hours notice may be subject to a fee.
